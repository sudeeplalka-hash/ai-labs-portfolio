INITIATIVE BRIEF · GENERATED no. 042 — insurance / claims

Stop paying for every mistake twice in claims adjudication.

I · THE SHARPENED PROBLEM

Claims adjusters re-key policy details across three systems; each wrong answer is paid for twice — once in rework, once in leakage. The unit of pain is the incorrectly settled claim, not the slow one. AI helps only if it is more correct than the current process, provably.

II · FALSIFIABLE HYPOTHESIS

“An assistant that drafts settlement rationales for one-click adjuster sign-off will cut error-driven leakage by 20% within one quarter — measured against a held-out golden set, not anecdotes.”

Falsified if: accuracy on the golden set < 85%, or adjusters override > 40% of drafts, or leakage moves < 5%.
